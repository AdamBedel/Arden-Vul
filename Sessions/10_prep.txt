What they can tell you:

Conversi (singular: conversus) are those members of the modern Priscian Order who are completely devoted to the
Priscian principles but lack the requisite training or ability in the arcane arts to become full members. Bonded
defenders are those conversi who have taken oaths to serve a particular, high-level circularius. 

Priscian circulariii (i.e., arcane spell-casters and full members of
the order) who undertake special missions outside a sept’s hold
are known as ‘war mages’.

War Mage Triads: When journeys or missions of particular importance
are deemed necessary by one of the Circles, a triad of war
mages is formed.
